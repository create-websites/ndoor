The Catalyst: An Act of Barbarism

On April 22, 2025, a brutal terror attack in Pahalgam, Kashmir, became the flashpoint for a decisive military response. The attack, which targeted unarmed tourists, was not just an act of violence but a calculated attempt to destabilize the region, compelling India to abandon its policy of strategic patience.
